2. Privacy Policy Effective Date: 18 February 2026 1. Overview ONE MISSION GROUP LTD. respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data within the MasterCourse iOS App. We operate under the principles of the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and the UK GDPR. 2. Data We Collect Usage Data: Standard Apple analytics (e.g., crash reports, device type) which are anonymized. User Content: Text inputs you type into the learning modules. Account Data: We do not store payment information on our servers; all transactions are processed securely by Apple. 3. How We Use Your Data We process your text inputs to provide educational responses. AI Processing: We utilize APIs from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google to generate responses. No Training: We have configured our API usage to ensure your data is not used to train these third-party models. Your inputs are used strictly for inference (generating the answer) and then discarded by the processor. 4. Data Retention We retain user-generated interaction logs (chat history and learning progress) for a maximum of 1 year to allow you to review your learning history. After this period, data is automatically deleted or anonymized. You may request earlier deletion by contacting support. 5. Third-Party Sharing We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with: Cloud Providers: For hosting our infrastructure (e.g., AWS/Google Cloud). AI Providers: As mentioned above, solely for generating responses. 6. Your Rights Under UK law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
